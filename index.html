Notes

The creation of 'Groove For Me' allowed me to experience how much you must immerse yourself into your studies in order to build a dream from the ground up. Having never built a game that runs in HTML5, and my scarce knowledge of implementing Javascript libraries into my code, it was quite an uphill battle in coding up a working product. New classes of problems seemed to rise up, primarily trading expensive features for performance. I believe that is where most of my effort went; finding a healthy mix of aesthetics and performance.

Upon the finalization of 'Groove For Me,' I feel I learned quite a bit and was able to push out a product that closely resembled my end goal, barred only by sacrificing features for performance gain. If I hadn't been encouraged to search for a fitting Javascript library for my next project, I may have never treaded into unfamiliar waters of game creation. It was a fulfilling experience, and gave me confidence to venture out in the future and implement new libraries into my projects.
